NATIONAL GREEN TRIBUNAL
Principal Bench, New Delhi
Original Application No. 45/2023

IN THE MATTER OF: Industrial Pollution in River Ganga

ORDER DATED: 10 March 2023

All industries discharging effluent into the Ganga river must:
1. Install real-time pollution monitoring systems
2. Maintain BOD below 30 mg/L and DO above 4 mg/L
3. CPCB standards BIS:10500 must be complied with

Violators will be charged environmental compensation of:
- ₹50,000 per day for first violation
- ₹1,00,000 per day for repeat violations
- Potential closure of unit

(NGT Order No. 45/2023)
