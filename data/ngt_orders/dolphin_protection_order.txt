NATIONAL GREEN TRIBUNAL
Order No. 2024/WL/61

Subject: Protection and Conservation of Gangetic Dolphins

ESTABLISHMENT OF DOLPHIN SANCTUARIES
This tribunal establishes protected zones for Gangetic dolphin conservation:
- Minimum 50km buffer zones from mining sites
- Seasonal breeding sanctuaries (June-August closure)
- Acoustic monitoring mandatory in all zones
- Dolphin population counting every 15 days

ENFORCEMENT MEASURES
1. Mining restrictions within 50km of known dolphin habitats
2. Vessel speed limits in sanctuary zones
3. Fishing net restrictions (mesh size > 10cm)
4. Trawling completely banned in Section 5 of all rivers
5. Chemical pollution monitoring at discharge points

PENALTIES
- Unauthorized mining in sanctuary: ₹50 lakh + 3 years imprisonment
- Vessel violations: ₹5 lakh fine per incident
- Dolphin harassment: ₹10 lakh + 2 years imprisonment
- False monitoring reports: ₹25 lakh + 1 year imprisonment

COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
- Monthly sanctuary inspection reports
- Audio recordings of hydrophone data
- Swimming behavior pattern analysis
- Population viability assessment every 60 days
- Third-party ecological audit twice yearly

LEGAL AUTHORITY
- Indian Wildlife Protection Act 1972, Schedule I (Gangetic dolphin)
- Environment Protection Act 1986, Section 3
- Biological Diversity Act 2002
- Ramsar Convention (wetland protection)

Order Date: 2024-02-15
Validity: Permanent until further order
Non-compliance: District Magistrate empowered for immediate cessation
