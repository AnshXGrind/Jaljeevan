NATIONAL GREEN TRIBUNAL
Principal Bench, New Delhi
Original Application No. 38/2024

IN THE MATTER OF: Illegal Sand Mining in River Ganga at Mirzapur

ORDER DATED: 15 January 2024

This Tribunal has taken suo motu cognizance of reports regarding
large-scale illegal sand mining in the Ganga riverbed at Mirzapur,
Uttar Pradesh. Such mining activities are in violation of:
1. Environment Impact Assessment Notification, 2006
2. Sand Mining Framework, 2018
3. Orders of the Hon'ble Supreme Court in WP(C) No. 435/2012

The Tribunal directs the District Magistrate, Mirzapur to:
a) Immediately stop all illegal mining activities
b) Seize equipment involved in illegal extraction
c) File FIR against violators under IPC Section 379
d) Submit action taken report within 2 weeks
